Hi, I am Bilal Kabaş. I am a third-year undergraduate student at Electrical-Electronics Engineering Department, at Abdullah Gül University. I have a great enthusiasm towards Robotics, AI, and computer vision. Currently, I am working on DRL-based navigation and path planning algorithms using single monocular camera. As a part of my capstone project, I am also working on object detection algorithms to develop an object detection/tracking-based SLAM algorithm. This research is conducted at Abdullah Gül University, Robotics Laboratory.
