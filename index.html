Hi, I am Bilal Kabaş. I am an undergraduate student at Electrical-Electronics Engineering Department, at Abdullah Gül University. I have a great enthusiasm towards Robotics. My focus is to develop decision making algorithms for autonomous robots. Currently, I am studying ML & DL and working on the development of an autonomous UAV capable of picking a load on a ground robot with high precision. The research is planned to be carried out at the Abdullah Gül University Robotics Laboratory.
